Pinpin-Eat Project

Pinpin-Eat is an Asian food delivery app in Montreal. With in one tap, you will have your local favourites sent directly to your door. The ordering process is very easy and user-friendly. Each user can walk through it effortlessly. All you need to do is just browsing the food menu, tap the item that user wants, add it to the cart and finish the ordering experience as quickly as possible.
